## the daily writing companion you might need but didn't know existed
just a simple page.
no folder. no multiple files. no distractions. just a simple place to temporarily dump thoughts and notes into. move them afterwards or just delete them if you are done.
the power of markdown.
no weird new file format. just simple and proven markdown. write in markdown and use all its amazing features. different headings. notes, tasks, and dones. and, highlights like bold, italic, linethrough and code.
vim and hotkeys.
its just you and your keyboard. level up your writing and flow through your notes with vim and hotkeys. give it a try. if you don't like vim, you can still deactivate it.
meta+b: bold
meta+j: italic
meta+k: code
meta+l: linethrough
meta+h: iterate headings
meta+shift+i: iterate note/task/done
